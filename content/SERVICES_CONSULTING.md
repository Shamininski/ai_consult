# Malon Labs Consulting — AI & IT Consulting Services

## Training Program Structure

### AI Literacy Foundation (All Tiers)
- **Duration:** 4-5 hours (1 day)
- **Audience:** All staff levels
- **Covers:**
  - What AI actually is (demystified, no jargon)
  - What AI can and cannot do — setting realistic expectations
  - Live demos of AI tools relevant to their industry
  - Hands-on exercises: writing effective prompts, getting useful outputs
  - Ethics, data privacy, and responsible AI use
  - When to trust AI output and when to verify

### Department-Specific Training (Tier 2+)
- **Duration:** 2-3 half-day sessions per department, spread across 1 week
- **Format:** 3 hours per session with practice between sessions
- **Why spread out:** Adults retain what they use the next day — marathon sessions don't work

## The Discovery Process (How We Adapt to Each Client)

### Step 1: AI Readiness Assessment
- Standardized questionnaire covering tech maturity, pain points, team readiness, budget
- Completed during or before the first meeting
- Gives us a baseline score and identifies quick wins

### Step 2: Workflow Mapping
- Sit with each department head (30-60 min each)
- Map actual daily workflows: What takes the most time? What's repetitive? What causes errors?
- Identify bottlenecks, single points of failure, manual processes ripe for automation

### Step 3: Opportunity Scoring
- Score each opportunity on: Impact (high/medium/low) × Ease of Implementation (easy/moderate/hard)
- Prioritize: High Impact + Easy Implementation = Phase 1 targets
- This matrix drives the implementation roadmap

### Step 4: Tool Matching
- Match each prioritized opportunity to specific AI tools
- Consider: cost, learning curve, integration with existing systems, internet requirements
- Always have a backup recommendation (in case of budget or connectivity constraints)

### Step 5: Pilot + Expand
- Start with 1-2 quick wins to build internal buy-in
- Measure results after 2-4 weeks
- Use success data to justify broader rollout
- Train internal champions who become AI advocates within the organization
