## How We Work

### Onboarding (Week 1-2)
1. IT audit — we document everything you currently have
2. Risk assessment — identify what's vulnerable or outdated
3. Priority fixes — address any critical issues immediately
4. Monitoring setup — install our management and monitoring tools
5. Documentation — create a complete IT asset inventory

### Ongoing Management
- Proactive monitoring catches problems before your staff notices them
- Regular maintenance windows for updates and patches
- Monthly reporting so leadership knows the state of their IT
- Quarterly reviews to plan improvements and budget

### Escalation Path
- Level 1: Remote helpdesk resolves common issues (password resets, software problems)
- Level 2: On-site technician for hardware or network issues
- Level 3: Senior engineer for complex infrastructure problems
- Level 4: Specialist escalation for security incidents or major outages

## Tanzania-Specific Considerations

- **Power reliability:** We plan for power interruptions. UPS systems, generator integration, and cloud-based failover are standard recommendations.
- **Internet connectivity:** We optimize for the bandwidth available. Caching, local hosting where appropriate, and failover ISP configurations.
- **Hardware sourcing:** We help navigate hardware procurement locally, avoiding import delays and ensuring warranty coverage.
- **Regulatory compliance:** We stay current with Tanzanian data protection regulations and industry-specific requirements.
- **Mobile workforce:** Many Tanzanian businesses have field staff. We support mobile device management and remote access solutions.
